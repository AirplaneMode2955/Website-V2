# Jett Iverson — Personal Website V2

Personal portfolio and professional website for Jett Iverson — marketer, husband, and AI strategist based in Utah.

## About

This site serves as a central hub for my professional work, projects, content, and personal life. It covers my background in performance marketing, SEO/GEO strategy, AI tool integration, and my experience as a Licensed Insurance Agent and Acting Director of Marketing at The Insurance Center.

## Sections

- **Hero** — Introduction and quick navigation
- **Professional Overview** — Career background, education, and certifications
- **Projects** — Featured work including a YouTube Thumbnail Generator and TIC Marketing Dashboard
- **Content** — Links to social media and content platforms
- **A Life in Balance** — A personal look at life outside of work
- **Contact** — Get in touch

## Tech Stack

- Plain HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — no frameworks or build tools
- [Tailwind CSS](https://tailwindcss.com/) via CDN
- Google Fonts — Newsreader & Manrope
- Material Symbols (Google Icons)

Professional Overview

At The Insurance Center of Utah, I led the development and execution of SEO and GEO strategies while overseeing the research, evaluation, and implementation of emerging AI tools to keep the organization at the forefront of marketing technology.

I focused on ensuring the brand appeared where it matters most — across both traditional search engines and AI-generated results — by aligning search optimization with generative engine optimization.
